# Specification

Fieldheld Site is a static public landing page for Fieldheld.

## Goals

- Present the current Fieldheld public positioning.
- Link to public GitHub surfaces only.
- Keep claims bounded to evidence infrastructure and synthetic public examples.

## Non-Goals

- Customer onboarding.
- Payment, analytics, forms, or account flows.
- Certification, compliance approval, or production safety claims.
- Non-public planning, roadmap, customer, or business notes.

## Public Boundary

The public surface is `index.html`, `styles.css`, and supporting repository docs. The site must not include secrets, personal contact details, customer data, private planning, or unpublished business direction.
